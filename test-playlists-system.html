🎯 How to Test Playlists

  1. Create a Playlist:
    • Go to "Çalma Listeleri" in sidebar
    • Click "Yeni Liste" button
    • Enter a playlist name
  2. Add Songs to Playlist:
    • Go to homepage or search
    • Hover over any song
    • Click the "+" button
    • Select your playlist
  3. View Playlist:
    • Go to "Çalma Listeleri"
    • Click on your playlist
    • See all songs in the playlist
  4. Play Playlist:
    • Open a playlist
    • Click "Tümünü Çal" button
    • All songs will play in order
  5. Manage Songs:
    • In playlist view, hover over songs
    • Click "×" to remove from playlist
  6. Delete Playlist:
    • In playlists view, hover over playlist
    • Click trash icon
    • Confirm deletion
